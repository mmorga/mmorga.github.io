Mexican Directors on Charlie Rose

Jan 4th, 2007 12:00 am | Comments

Alejandro Gonzalez Inarritu director of “Babel”, Alfonso Cuaron, director of “Children of Men”, and one of my favorite directors, Guillermo del Toro, of “Pan’s Labyrinth”, sit down with Charlie Rose and discuss their work. I haven’t seen “Babel” yet (though it looks interesting), but I’m dying to see “Children of Men” and especially “Pan’s Labyrinth”. Which I plan on driving to Austin to see on January 12th. Any other takers? P.S. If anyone reading this (is anyone reading this?) hasn’t seen The Devil’s Backbone run out and buy a copy on DVD ASAP. I guarantee you will love it. It’s one of the best ghost stories that I’ve ever seen.
